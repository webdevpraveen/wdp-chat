About Us

Short Intro

SRMU Web Chat is a minimal, fast, and completely anonymous platform designed for open communication.

We believe in privacy by default. No accounts, emails, or phone numbers are ever required to start chatting.

Every session is temporary, and you're assigned a unique random ID each time you connect.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. We do not store chat messages on our servers permanently.

We do not collect personal identifiers, IP addresses in logs, or device fingerprints.

Connections are powered by secure WebSockets, and data exists only in-memory during active sessions.

No cookies, no trackers, and no third-party analytics are used here.

Terms of Use

This platform is a tool for free speech and connection. However, illegal activities, harassment, and harmful behavior are strictly prohibited.

Users are solely responsible for the content they share. The developer is not liable for any misuse of the platform.

Please maintain a respectful environment for everyone.

Technology

Built with Node.js, Express, Socket.io, and Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS. Hosted on Render (Backend) and Vercel (Frontend).
